Pan Seared Chicken Thighs

Description:

This recipe will show you how to pan sear a boneless skinless chicken thigh.

Recipe:

  • Boneless Chicken Thigh
  • Onion Powder
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Paprika
  • Turmeric
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Chicken broth

Instructions

  1. Measure the spices and mix them together
  2. Pat chicken thigh very dry with a paper towel
  3. Rub seasoning on chicken thigh with your hands
  4. Coat bottom of the pan with olive oil, and make sure the oil is hot
  5. Place chicken thigh with smooth side down first
  6. Cook for at least 5-7 minutes, and flip it on the other side
  7. Cook it until the juices run clear or chicken is 160-165 degrees farenheit
