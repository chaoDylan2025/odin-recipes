Oven-roasted-asparagus


Description:

This dish goes well with a protein of your choice, and it is easy to make!

Recipe:

  • Bunch of thin asparagus spears, trimmed
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1.5 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese (opitional)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • .5 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice (Optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F
  2. Place asparagus into a mixing bowl; drizzle with olive oil and toss to coat.
    Sprinkle with parmesan cheese, garlic, salt, and pepper.
    Arrange them in a single layer in a baking dish before putting them in the oven
  3. Bake in preheated oven until just tender, 12-15 minutes depening on thickness
    Sprinkle with lemon juice before serving/li>
